# Data Science Salary Analysis (2020–2025)

## Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze salary trends in data science roles from 2020 to 2025 and understand how different factors influence salary levels.



## Dataset Description

The dataset contains information about data science jobs across multiple years.
Key variables include:

- Work year
- Job title
- Experience level
- Salary in USD
- Company size
- Company location



## Tools Used

1. Python
2. NumPy
3. Pandas
4. Matplotlib
5. Seaborn
6. Jupyter Notebook



## Analysis Overview

The analysis is divided into three parts:

### 1. Univariate Analysis

Analysis of individual variables such as salary distribution and yearly trends.

### 2. Bivariate Analysis

Study of relationships between salary and one other variable, including experience level, job title, and company size.

### 3. Multivariate Analysis

Analysis of salary influenced by multiple factors simultaneously, such as:

- Experience level and job title
- Company size and company location
- Salary trends over time by job title



## Key Findings

- Salary increases significantly with higher experience levels.
- Senior and Executive roles earn higher median salaries.
- Job title plays a major role in salary variation.
- Company location and company size impact salary levels.
- Salaries have generally increased over the years.



## Conclusion

This project provides insights into the factors affecting salaries in data science roles. The findings can help in understanding market trends and career progression within the data science field.
